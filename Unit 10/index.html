Traveling makes you a better person!

Getting outside of your bubbles and learning more about other cultures can help a person gain emotional maaturity and empthay. It's easy to believe the stereotypes around us and accept them as truth when we don't have any data to show us otherwise. Traveling is so important becausee it lets people expand their world view and grow.

Life satisfaction tends to go up after a person has traveled more, epecially if that person has traveled to a less wealthy nation. In the documentary "Happy" the narrator discusses the disparity between how much money someone makes and how highly they rate their life satisfaction. It turns out that after you have enough cash to take care of your basic needs like food and water, more money doesn't always lead to a happier life. A lot of the happiest people in the world have very little. Perhaps, that helps them focus on what really matters in life, like relationships.

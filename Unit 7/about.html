About

Why Did I Make Campus Cooking?

When I first moved away for college, I felt a sense of panic. That was for a lot of reasons, but being an adult, all on your own for the first time is very overwelming. There are a lot of new stresses that fall under that category, but for me, food was a big one. I was lucky to have had a lovely mother all of my life who always made meals for our family. When I moved away, I quickly realized that I needed to figure out how to cook for myself and fast.

As I have learned more about food and the college experience, I feel that I have really come into my own culinary style. I'm really excited to share some of what I have picked up and hopefully make cooking on campus a little bit easier for you!
